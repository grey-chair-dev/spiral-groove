1. Brand Foundation
🎯 Brand Purpose
To preserve and celebrate the analog warmth of vinyl culture while connecting collectors, music lovers, and community members through an authentic and welcoming record shop experience — both in-store and online.

💬 Mission Statement
To make discovering music feel human again — combining the tactile joy of vinyl with a seamless modern experience.

❤️ Core Values
Value

Meaning

Authenticity

Everything we sell, share, and recommend comes from genuine love for music.

Community

We’re more than a shop — we’re a local hub for creativity and connection.

Craftsmanship

We take pride in curating records, events, and experiences with care.

Simplicity

From store layout to website design, we keep things clear and accessible.

Warmth

Every touchpoint — visual or human — should feel like home for music lovers.

🧠 Brand Personality
Friendly • Nostalgic • Artistic • Passionate • Approachable

✍️ Tone of Voice
Warm & Conversational: Feels like chatting with your favorite record store owner.

Knowledgeable but Unpretentious: Expertise without ego.

Nostalgic with Modern Edge: Vintage spirit, current sensibility.

📍 Brand Positioning Statement
For vinyl enthusiasts and casual music lovers alike, Spiral Groove Records offers a curated, community-driven experience that blends classic analog charm with the convenience and accuracy of modern retail — creating a space where music truly connects people.

2. Visual Identity Development
🔵 Primary Logo
Design Essence: Hand-lettered, circular “groove” motif evokes vinyl record texture and community connection.

Color Usage: White text with red outline and teal accent for “RECORDS.”

Personality: Artistic, vintage, and organic — perfectly aligned with the brand’s analog ethos.

🔴 Secondary Logo Concepts (Recommended Extensions)
SG Monogram: Compact badge version for favicon, stickers, or merch tags.

Wordmark: Horizontal “Spiral Groove Records” in Playfair Display or similar retro serif font for headers and banners.

Badge Stamp: Circular stamp logo (white on black) for vinyl sleeves and tote bags.

3. Brand Guidelines
✅ Logo Usage
Do

Don’t

Use logo on black, cream, or teal backgrounds

Stretch, skew, or crop the logo

Maintain circular integrity with minimum padding (25px clear space)

Place over busy or clashing backgrounds

Use solid white logo on dark photos for watermarking

Alter color of “RECORDS” accent text

Minimum Sizes:

Web: 120px height

Print: 1.25” diameter minimum

📸 Imagery & Photography Style
Mood: Warm, cozy, analog lighting — natural shadows and imperfections.

Subjects:

Customers browsing crates

Close-up shots of record textures, turntables, album art

Event photos with soft lights, candid smiles

Color Grading: Soft film grain, warm tones, slight vignette for depth.

Lighting: Natural or tungsten — never fluorescent or cold.

6. Brand Application Strategy
🖥️ Digital Touchpoints
Website: Consistent typography, color palette, and spacing grid.

Social Media: Logo badge avatar, teal & amber overlays, consistent filters.

Email Campaigns: Retro header banner, cream background, teal CTAs.

🛍️ Physical Touchpoints
In-store signage, bags, stickers using circular logo.

Event posters with retro typography and amber highlights.

Record sleeve stamps using secondary logo in white or black.
