1. Project Overview
🎯 Purpose
Spiral Groove Records’ new website will transform their online presence into a high-performing digital storefront that accurately reflects their brand’s analog warmth, drives online and in-store sales, and positions the business as a community hub for vinyl culture in the Greater Cincinnati area.

🧭 Primary Business Goals
Sync Square POS inventory to ensure real-time accuracy between in-store and online stock.

Increase online sales and conversions through simplified UX and high-performing CTAs.

Showcase and monetize the event space with dedicated subpages and inquiry automation.

Enhance visibility and credibility through SEO, Google Business, and local partnerships.

Grow the customer base and loyalty via email signups, automation, and repeat engagement.

3. Visual Design Requirements
🎨 Overall Direction
Warm analog nostalgia meets clean digital simplicity. Design should evoke the feeling of flipping through records in-store — tactile, inviting, and uncluttered.

Element

Specification

Style

Retro-modern minimalism; spacious layout; focus on imagery and typography

Color Palette

Deep black, cream, muted teal, amber highlights

Typography

Headings: Playfair Display (retro charm); Body: Inter or Poppins (modern legibility)

Imagery

Lifestyle and product photography with warm tones and analog textures

Logo Usage

Circular hand-lettered logo preferred; maintain round geometry; no skewing/stretching

Design Priority

Credibility, warmth, and clear user flow; all elements should drive conversion or engagement

5. UX/UI Guidelines
🧠 Experience Principles
Mobile-First: Prioritize touch-friendly design and vertical scrolling.

Conversion-Focused:

Persistent cart icon and “Shop New Arrivals” button.

Exit-intent popups for email signups.

Streamlined checkout with 3 steps max.

Accessibility:

WCAG 2.1 AA compliance.

Minimum contrast ratio 4.5:1.

Keyboard navigation support.

Alt text and ARIA tags on all media.

🖱️ Key UX Patterns
Sticky header with navigation and search bar.

Homepage “New Arrivals” auto-collection updated weekly.

Quick filters in Shop view for faster browsing.

Visual consistency across all devices.

8. SEO & Marketing Requirements
🔍 On-Page SEO
Title & meta descriptions per page (≤ 60/155 chars).

Keyword inclusion in headers and first 100 words.

Alt attributes on all images.

Internal linking (Home → Shop → Product → Event).

Canonical tags to prevent duplication.

📍 Local SEO
Embed Google Map + “Milford, OH” schema.

Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across all pages.

Google Business posts and event sync.

🧠 Content Marketing
2 blogs per month (vinyl tips + local music features).

Monthly email newsletter.

Seasonal promotions (Record Store Day, holidays).

📊 Analytics & Tracking
GA4: event tracking (cart adds, form submissions).

Search Console: index coverage & keyword insights.

Hotjar: scroll depth, heatmaps for conversion optimization.
