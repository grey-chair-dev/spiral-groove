1. Executive Summary
Spiral Groove Records has built a beloved local brand rooted in community, music, and authenticity. However, the current website and digital presence do not fully capture that energy — or convert it into consistent online sales and event inquiries.

This proposal outlines a complete digital transformation designed to:

Accurately sync online inventory with in-store stock through Square POS integration.

Create a clean, intuitive website that invites browsing and discovery.

Showcase the shop’s growing event space and community presence.

Build a foundation for sustainable growth through SEO, social media, and email marketing.

The result will be a warm, modern, and high-performing website that reflects the soul of Spiral Groove — and drives measurable business growth through online and in-person engagement.

2. Market Opportunity & Impact
🎯 Market Context
The vinyl resurgence is booming — with U.S. vinyl record sales surpassing 43 million units in 2024, marking the 18th consecutive year of growth. Local record stores remain the heartbeat of this movement, offering something digital platforms can’t: connection, curation, and community.

Milford and the Greater Cincinnati area boast a passionate base of music lovers, collectors, and gift buyers seeking authenticity and a physical experience — exactly what Spiral Groove offers. Yet, much of this audience discovers new stores online first.

💡 Opportunity
A modern, optimized website will allow Spiral Groove to:

Capture new local and national customers searching for vinyl online.

Turn browsers into buyers with accurate, real-time stock and easy checkout.

Grow event revenue through a professional event-space page and booking system.

Build loyalty through content, email marketing, and social engagement.

📈 Expected Impact
With the right foundation, Spiral Groove can expect:

30–60% increase in online sales within 6 months of launch.

40% increase in event space inquiries.

25–35% increase in store visits via Google and social visibility.

3. Proposed Solutions
🌐 Website Development & Design
Custom-designed site that blends analog warmth with digital simplicity.

Square POS integration for real-time inventory sync and accurate product listings.

Full eCommerce functionality (Shop, New Arrivals, Coming Soon, Staff Picks).

Event Space subpage with gallery, FAQs, inquiry form, and calendar.

Mobile-first, fast-loading design optimized for all screen sizes.

🔍 SEO & Content Strategy
Keyword optimization for “vinyl records Milford OH,” “record stores near Cincinnati,” etc.

Local SEO integration via Google Business Profile and map embeds.

“Groove Notes” blog to highlight new arrivals, staff picks, and local artist stories.

Schema markup for products and events to improve search visibility.

🧭 UX/UI & Conversion Optimization
Streamlined browsing with filters for genre, decade, and condition.

Persistent “Shop New Arrivals” button for quick access.

Newsletter signup at key conversion points.

Event inquiry form with auto-confirmation email.

Accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1) and performance optimization.

📱 Social Media & Google Business Optimization
Setup and optimize Google Business Profile (photos, events, reviews, Q&A).

Create consistent Instagram, Facebook, and TikTok branding.

Plan monthly content themes (“New Arrivals,” “Staff Picks,” “Upcoming Events”).

Add Google Posts for new inventory and events to boost local visibility.

⚙️ Marketing Automation & Lead Generation
Integrate Square + Mailchimp for automated emails and segmented lists.

Launch “Join the Groove” campaign with a 10% first-purchase incentive.

Set up email automations for new arrivals and loyalty members.

CRM tagging for collectors, event-goers, and gift buyers.
