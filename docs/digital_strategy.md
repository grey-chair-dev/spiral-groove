1. Website Strategy & Planning
🎯 Primary Purpose
To create a clean, modern, analog-inspired website that accurately reflects in-store inventory, drives online and local sales, highlights community events, and strengthens Spiral Groove’s reputation as a regional vinyl hub.

🧩 Core Website Goals
Accuracy & Trust: Sync Square POS inventory for real-time updates.

Conversion: Simplify product browsing and checkout flow.

Community Engagement: Promote event space, local collaborations, and newsletter signups.

Discovery & Growth: Drive organic traffic through local SEO and “vinyl culture” content.

Scalability: Future-ready foundation for eCommerce, events, and loyalty programs

3. UX/UI & Conversion Planning
🧭 User Journey
Awareness: Arrives via search/social → Home or Blog.

Interest: Browses New Arrivals or Shop filters.

Decision: Adds product to cart or inquires about events.

Action: Completes checkout or submits inquiry form.

Retention: Receives follow-up emails for new releases & events.

🖱️ Conversion Optimization
Persistent “Shop New Arrivals” button on every page.

Newsletter signup at footer + post-purchase thank-you popup.

“Customers Also Bought” suggestions using Square categories.

Simple checkout: 3 steps max, mobile-friendly.

📱 Mobile-First & Accessibility
70%+ users likely mobile → prioritize one-handed scrolling, large buttons.

WCAG-compliant color contrast & alt text.

Lazy-load images for speed.

Hand-lettered circular “Spiral Groove Records” logo on dark backgrounds; avoid flattening or skewing

Consistent logo placement (top-left desktop, center mobile).

Primary CTA color: muted teal or orange accent.

Background: textured off-white or matte black.
